# DermoScanners User Guide

Welcome to DermoScanners! This guide will help you get the most out of your skin health companion.

## üöÄ Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
- Visit the app and click "Get Started"
- Fill in your name, email, and password
- Add your skin type (dry, oily, combination, sensitive, or normal)
- Describe your skincare goals (minimum 10 characters)

### 2. Complete Your Profile
- Navigate to your Profile page
- Update your skin type for better recommendations
- Set specific skincare goals (e.g., "reduce acne", "anti-aging", "hydration")
- Your profile completion percentage will increase as you add more information

## üîç Using the AI Scanner

### Scanning Skin Lesions
1. Click "Scan" in the navigation menu
2. Choose "Skin Scan" mode
3. Upload a clear photo of the skin lesion
   - Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP
   - Maximum file size: 5MB
4. Click "Analyze Image"
5. Wait ~3 seconds for AI analysis
6. View your results with confidence score

### Understanding Results
- **Benign**: Low risk, routine monitoring recommended
- **Suspicious**: Moderate risk, consult a dermatologist
- **Malignant**: High risk, seek immediate medical attention

**Important**: This tool is for informational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice.

## üìú Scan History

### Viewing History
- Click "History" to see all your scans
- Each scan shows:
  - Result classification
  - Confidence score
  - Processing time
  - Timestamp

### Managing History
- **Delete Individual Scans**: Click the trash icon
- **Download Backup**: Save your history as JSON file
- **Upload Backup**: Restore from a previous backup

### Backup & Restore
Your scan history is automatically saved to:
1. **Local Storage**: Instant access, works offline
2. **Cloud Database**: Synced across devices

To backup manually:
1. Go to History page
2. Click "Download Backup"
3. Save the JSON file securely

To restore:
1. Click "Upload Backup"
2. Select your backup JSON file
3. Your scans will be restored

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Upload Issues
- **File too large**: Compress image or use lower resolution
- **Invalid format**: Convert to JPEG, PNG, or WebP
- **Upload failed**: Check internet connection and retry

### Slow Performance
- Clear browser cache
- Close unnecessary tabs
- Check internet speed
- Try a different browser

### Missing Recommendations
- Complete your profile
- Adjust filter settings
- Clear filters and try again
- Check if products are in database

### Sync Issues
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Download backup as precaution
- Contact support if persists
